What is We Ignite Tech Program?


It is a full stack developer program on speed with the aim to enable newcomers to enter the tech job market faster, as well as helping businesses find new talented techies.

The program is for people with some prior knowledge and experience with programming, who want to refresh and update their knowledge to meet the requirements of the labour market. So join us and be part of the cool WE IGNITE TEAM!

Are you, or someone you know who is the right candidate? Keep reading :)

HOW DOES IT WORK?


In the span of 4 months, you will improve your technical skills and learn what is required of you to work as a developer in Norway. The program will combine practical training in programming, development of your personal skills, build professional network and give you insight into relevant job opportunities.

Customized to candidate with some prior tech experience

5 - 10 students

Evening classes 2 times/week + homework

*Free of charge

**Limited number of spots.

WHAT WILL YOU GAIN?


TECHNICAL TRAINING:

Practical programming classes

Homework: theory and online training

Introduction to IT architecture, data security, software delivery and DevOps

SOFT SKILLS TRAINING


Soft skill training e.g. teamwork, leadership, communication

Relevant professional mentors and network

Meet Norwegian employers looking for techies

Introduction to innovation methods


Everyone will get a We Ignite Tech certificate.



WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR?


GENERAL CRITERIA

Newcomers: arrived in the past five years with residence permit (temporary or permanent).

Over 18 years old

Living in the Oslo area

Good level in Norwegian and/or English

Higher education is not a requirement, but an advantage

You should be prepared to dedicate at least 15-20 hours per week (6 hours in class + practical work on your own and/or with fellow students).


TECHNICAL QUALIFICATIONS

Technology background (education or equivalent experience) (NOT: hardware)

Coding experience or good understanding of statistics and/or maths.

Some technologies you should be familiar with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Nice to have: some basic experience with a server-side programming language (C, C#, Go, Java, Python, Ruby)


PERSONAL CHARACTERISTICS:

Motivated and engaged, positive, focused on teamwork, takes responsibility for your own learning and career development and willing to work hard to reach your goals.
