What Is We Ignite Tech?

We Ignite Tech is a full stack developer program for newcomers in Norway. The aim is to contribute to raising the competence levels of newcomers with IT-background to ensure they become more relevant as employees for Norwegian businesses. Both through programming skills and soft skills.

We aim to help businesses with their recruitment and inclusion. And we want to enable newcomers to build a relevant professional network and get closer to relevant job opportunities. Newcomers have experience, higher education, good ideas, initiatives and inspiration that are valuable for Norwegian businesses. There is a huge demand for developers and digital heads. Meanwhile, newcomers with valuable tech background are not given the opportunity to update their practical knowledge to meet the requirements of the market and be part of the global tech community. Our mission is to find solutions to bridge that gap: the We Ignite Tech program.
