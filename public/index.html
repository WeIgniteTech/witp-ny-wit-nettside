The We Ignite Tech program

A full stack developer program on speed with the aim to enable newcomers to enter the tech job market faster, as well as helping businesses find new talented techies.

Full Stack

The program helps people with a technical background to become operational full stack developer.

Accurate

The technologies and prosesses we teach are those of the tech community.

Community

The students grow in a group and shape a new community.

Soft-skills

The program offers training in a various set of non-tecnical skills.

Portfolio

Students build their own portfolio by making the result of their work available online.

Coaching

Students are in contact with a community of persons coaching them through the program.
